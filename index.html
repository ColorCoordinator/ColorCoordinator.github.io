ColorCoordinator

A mobile solution to coordinating your wardrobe

Our Mission

ColorCoordinator is a mobile app designed to help you mix and match your wardrobe to make the best outfit possible! Our mission is for ColorCoordinator to be your personal advisor to ensure that everything you're wearing works to make one cohesive outfit that matches perfectly. Whether you have a visual impairment preventing you from seeing true colors, or you just don't have an instinct for what colors go together best, ColorCoordinator will rate your outfit and help you decide what to wear.

Features

  • Quickly compare the items of your outfit using your phone's camera
  • Matches your clothing based on color theory and gives you a match percentage
  • When your outfit doesn't match, you get a suggestion for a better color
  • Think the rating is too strict? You're allowed to lower or raise the match threshold!

Accessibility

Accessibility is important to us, so we made sure to design ColorCoordinator with this in mind. Audio and visual cues are included to allow anyone easy use of the app. However, if you do not wish to use audio cues, they can be turned off in the settings menu.
